Health Is Wealth

Health is wealth, this proverb is one of the most famous proverb in the world. Which means that there is health there is wealth. Health body overall well being that is physically, mentally, emotionally, and socially. A healthy body is free from any disease and is very positive in their attitude. There is nothing in our life that is most valuable than good health. Without health there is no happiness, no peace, no success. A person with bad health cannot enjoy the pleasure of being wealthy. You can live without good wealth but without good health its impossible to survive. So,it is very important that everyone to maintain their health and to get free from ill and feel good all the time. It is possible when you can sleep at the right time and sleep at least 8 hours daily. Because sleep is an essential role in your health. To maintain a proper time to eat light food, exercise daily, sleep 9-10 PM, and wake up by 6 am daily. We should have balanced diet having the right proportion of carbohydrates, proteins, minerals, and vitamins. Avoid habits like sleep late night, smoking, drinking alcohol. Avoid eating junk food and street food. A person who is healthy mind can achieve success easily than an unhealthy person. Good health is the greatest blessing.
